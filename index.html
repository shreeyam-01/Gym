Perfect Gym Routine

Monday: Cardio

Cardio workouts help improve cardiovascular health, burn calories, and boost endurance.

Wednesday: Upper Body and Core

Upper body and core workouts help build upper body strength and enhance core stability.

Thursday: Active Rest and Recovery

Rest and recovery days are essential for muscle repair and overall well-being.

Friday: Lower Body with Focus on Glutes

Targeting glutes improves lower body aesthetics, strength, and functionality.

Saturday: Upper Body

Continuing upper body workouts for balanced strength and muscle development.

Sunday: Rest and Recovery

Another day dedicated to rest and recovery to allow your body to heal and recharge.
